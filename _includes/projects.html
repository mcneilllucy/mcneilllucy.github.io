About me

Currently* I am working as a postdoctoral researcher in RIKEN's Interdisciplinary Theoretical and Mathematical Sciences Program (iTHEMS).

Work history

Until October 2023, I worked as a JSPS fellow in theoretical astrophysics, in the Department of Physics at Kyoto University.

During Oct 2020-Sept 2021, I developed the Bioconductor package synapsis under the supervision of Dr. Wayne Crismani.

In May 2021 I was awarded a PhD in astrophysics from Monash University in Melbourne, under the supervision of Dr. Bernhard Müller.
